About Marlene<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Marlene grew up on an organic vegetable farm in Canada where most of her family still resides.Perhaps that\u2019s the origins of her love for nature and the land.<\/span><\/p>

\u00a0<\/span><\/p>

Currently she lives and works in Colorado Springs and Denver and couldn\u2019t be happier to call the mountain state home. Three grown children and her husband are her greatest gifts and the
heartbeat of her life.<\/p>

Artist Statement<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Thank goodness art is a continuously creative and new adventure, because the change is what Marlene thrives on.<\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Always having art as her creative outlet, her work became most concentrated in the early 90\u2019s primarily in the the pastel medium. Landscapes all around Colorado provided stunning inspiration for hundreds of paintings.<\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

During the last decade, her appetite has been whet by the desire to study more comprehensively the techniques and tools of the old masters. Artists and art lovers worldwide have grown in appreciation of the mastery of this kind of work and Marlene currently directs an art school in Denver with this as their mission.<\/p>

\u00a0<\/p>

Currently her chosen medium is oils and the endless opportunities to layer and glaze are nourishment for the creative soul.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
